## Identity

A university-grade unsupervised machine-learning curriculum that teaches clustering, dimensionality reduction, density estimation, and representation learning from first principles.

## Vision

A complete unsupervised learning curriculum — the natural companion to the supervised series, same rigour, same pedagogy, same production standards.

## Mission

Derives the mathematics, implements from scratch in NumPy, and applies production Scikit-learn/PyTorch — from K-means through autoencoders.
